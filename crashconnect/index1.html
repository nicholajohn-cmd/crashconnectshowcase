About Us

Who We
Are.

Crash Connect Pvt Ltd is building CATS — an advanced AI-powered safety solution that automatically detects accidents and instantly alerts emergency services, even when you can't ask for help.

We are Crash Connect Pvt Ltd — building an intelligent accident detection and emergency response system that connects vehicles, people, and emergency services in real time. Our mission is simple — reduce response time and save lives.

🚨
The ProblemEvery year, thousands of lives are lost due to delayed emergency response. Many victims are unable to call for help due to injuries, shock, or unconsciousness.
⚡
Our SolutionCrash Connect's CATS acts instantly without waiting for human intervention — combining IoT hardware, AI-based detection, and real-time communication.
🌍
The VisionMake CATS the safety standard in every Indian vehicle. Crash Connect Pvt Ltd is committed to reducing accident fatalities and making roads safer for everyone.
